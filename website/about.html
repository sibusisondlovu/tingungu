We Are Tingungu

Our purpose, values, and beliefs.

Tingungu is a welcoming community of faith committed to worshiping God, growing in Christlikeness, and serving our neighbourhood. We meet to celebrate God’s love, hear Scripture taught clearly, and care for one another as a family of believers.

Our Purpose.

We exist to proclaim the gospel, make disciples and demonstrate Christ’s compassion in practical ways. Through worship, teaching and outreach we seek to point people to Jesus and equip them to live out their faith in every area of life.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to connect people to God and one another, to train followers of Jesus for service, and to bring hope to our community. We pursue faithful Bible teaching, intentional discipleship and partnership in local ministry.

"For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him."

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Core Values

Our core values shape how we worship, disciple, and serve. We are committed to helping people find their identity in Christ and investing in a family culture.

Identity in Christ

We believe our primary identity and worth are found in Jesus. Our ministries help people discover and live out their new identity through gospel-centred teaching and discipleship.

Church Is Family

We cultivate deep, lasting relationships where members care for one another, celebrate together, and carry each other's burdens as a spiritual family.

Kingdom Culture

We seek to live out the priorities of God's kingdom—justice, mercy, humility and generosity—so our community reflects Jesus' love in practical ways.

Spirit Led Life

We depend on the Holy Spirit for guidance, power and transformation, prioritizing prayer, spiritual growth and obedience in all we do.

What We Believe

Confessional foundations that anchor our theology, teaching, and church body.

The Bible — God's Word

We believe the Bible is God’s inspired, authoritative and trustworthy revelation to humanity. It is our guide for faith and life.

God — Father, Son and Spirit

We confess one true God existing in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, the only Savior.

Salvation — By Grace Through Faith

Salvation is a gift of God received by faith in Jesus Christ. We are saved by grace through repentance and trust in Christ’s atoning work.

The Church — Community and Mission

The church is God’s gathered people, called to worship, disciple and serve. We practice baptism and the Lord’s Supper, and join him in mission.
